Don't Trust

A multitude of distrusts shape participants' experiences of the lead crisis. A deep distrust of government systems is intertwined with the distrust of their infrastructure, their home environment, and even their children's toys. The suspicion is that lead could be anywhere, and as there is no definitive list of safe or unsafe places and things, it's best to assume that nothing can be trusted.
