Teaching the Next Generation

The lead crisis in Milwaukee has persisted through multiple generations. Participants' do not trust in the government's willingness or ability to end the lead epidemic, so they believe that the only way to protect their children (and future generations beyond them) is to teach kids how to keep themselves safe from lead.
