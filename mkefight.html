Always Having to Fight

Coping with lead exposure on a daily basis is a constant battle. It is exhausting and traumatizing in a hundred different ways. From people's efforts to reduce exposure in any way they can, to the struggle of getting an education when you have learning disabilities, to the physical and behavioral effects of lead poisoning that have to be managed on an ongoing basis, we can see that battle in these images.
